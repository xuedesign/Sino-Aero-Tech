Aerospace LED Lights

● Aircraft Navigation Lights – Position Lights:
The aircraft navigation lights help pilots to determine if other aircrafts are coming or going, and their relative position and direction.

● Steady Lights:
The left wing tip has a steady red LED light, and the right wing tip has a steady green LED light. The tail has a steady white LED light. These navigation or position lights are steady (non flashing) lights, and they must be visible from 2 miles. We produce a variety of LED lights, which are energy- saving, excellent identification, and are ideal for airfield or aircraft lighting.
