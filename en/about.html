Company Profile

• Along with the vigorous development of aircraft, enplaning is one of the first choices of long distance transportation. It is no longer a dream to drive by yourself and hover over the sky during the advanced 21st century.
• It had over 30 years of history for introducing the Light Sport Aircraft in Taiwan. In recently years, the government stipulates the related regulations, assists establishing Light Sport Aircraft professional clubs, provides complete facilities and promotes the certification system in expectation to provide a more proactive vision for Light Sport Aircraft Industry.
• Currently, the existing Light Sport Aircrafts are all imported. Aside from the existing and affirmative supply chains of the global light sport aircrafts, the huge R&D cost pulls the investors back from devoting into aerospace industry. We aimed at developing and designing a Made-in-Taiwan Light Sport Aircraft.
• Sino Aerotech Co., Ltd. was established in March, 2015. We've collaborated a group of professional team members and started designing and assembling with the utilization of advanced composite material for our first Light Sport Aircraft. We look forward to promoting the name of Taiwan onto the stage of aerospace industry.
